Rotate

Rotates the image, for straightening the horizon or a dominant vertical line. The slider rotates the preview image in .1 degree increments. The present implementation is limited to rotations from -45 to +45 degrees.

The autocrop checkbox enables automatic post-rotate cropping of the image to maximum usable image; the preview shows the boundaries on the uncropped rotated image.

Properties: See tool.rotate.* in Configuration Properties
